Title: Searches for bridged bicyclic molecules in space—norbornadiene and its cyano derivatives

Authors: Marie-Aline Martin-Drumel, Jean-Thibaut Spaniol, Helen Hölzel, Marcelino Agúndez, Jose Cernicharo

Journal/Conference: Faraday Discussions

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/d3fd00016h

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/d3fd00016h

Abstract:
<jats:p>Laboratory rotational spectra of norbornadiene and its cyano derivatives were recorded using chirped-pulse millimetre-wave spectroscopy. These molecules were then searched for in the starless core TMC-1 using the QUIJOTE line survey.</jats:p>
