Title: Photon upconverting bioplastics with high efficiency and in-air durability

Authors: Pankaj Bharmoria, Shota Hisamitsu, Yoichi Sasaki, Tejwant Singh Kang, Masa-aki Morikawa

Journal/Conference: Journal of Materials Chemistry C

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/d1tc00287b

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/d1tc00287b

Abstract:
<jats:p>The accommodation of chromophore-dissolved microdroplets in semicrystalline protein matrices succeeds in achieving upconverting bioplastics with high efficiency, air-stability, and long-term durability for the first time.</jats:p>
