Title: Ionic liquid based battery electrolytes using lithium and sodium pseudo-delocalized pyridinium anion salts

Authors: Elham Hosseini-Bab-Anari, Adriana M. Navarro-Suárez, Kasper Moth-Poulsen, Patrik Johansson

Journal/Conference: Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/c9cp03445e

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/c9cp03445e

Abstract:
<p>Ionic liquid based electrolytes using a novel pseudo-delocalized anion – 1-methylpyridinum 2,6-dicarboxylate (MM26py) – are presented and tested for modern battery usage.</p>
