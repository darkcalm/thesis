Title: Triplet–triplet annihilation based near infrared to visible molecular photon upconversion

Authors: Pankaj Bharmoria, Hakan Bildirir, Kasper Moth-Poulsen

Journal/Conference: Chemical Society Reviews

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/d0cs00257g

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/d0cs00257g

Abstract:
<p>This review delineates the developments in triplet–triplet annihilation based NIR to Vis molecular photon upconversion including recent progress in conceptual design, applications, existing challenges, possible future directions and opportunities.</p>
