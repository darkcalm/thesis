Title: Enhancing the statistical probability factor in triplet–triplet annihilation photon upconversion
                    <i>via</i>
                    TIPS functionalization

Authors: Lukas Naimovičius, Manvydas Dapkevičius, Edvinas Radiunas, Mila Miroshnichenko, Gediminas Kreiza

Journal/Conference: Chemical Science

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/d5sc05248c

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/d5sc05248c

Abstract:
<jats:p>A new green-emitting annihilator, TIPS-PY is synthesized, which exhibits the high TTA-UC quantum yield of 13.7% for red-to-green upconversion due to the enhancement of spin statistical probability factor by TIPS functionalization of perylene.</jats:p>
