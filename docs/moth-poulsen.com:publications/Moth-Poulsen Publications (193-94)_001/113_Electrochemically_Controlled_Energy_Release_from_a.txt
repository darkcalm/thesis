Title: Electrochemically controlled energy release from a norbornadiene-based solar thermal fuel: increasing the reversibility to 99.8% using HOPG as the electrode material

Authors: Fabian Waidhas, Martyn Jevric, Michael Bosch, Tian Yang, Evanie Franz

Journal/Conference: Journal of Materials Chemistry A

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/d0ta00377h

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/d0ta00377h

Abstract:
<p>Solar energy conversion using molecular photoswitches holds great potential to store energy from sunlight in the form of chemical energy in a process that can be easily implemented in a direct solar energy storage device.</p>
