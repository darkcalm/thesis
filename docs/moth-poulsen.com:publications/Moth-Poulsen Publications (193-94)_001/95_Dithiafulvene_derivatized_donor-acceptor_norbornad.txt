Title: Dithiafulvene derivatized donor–acceptor norbornadienes with redshifted absorption

Authors: Mads Mansø, Martin Drøhse Kilde, Sandeep Kumar Singh, Paul Erhart, Kasper Moth-Poulsen

Journal/Conference: Physical Chemistry Chemical Physics

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/c8cp07744d

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/c8cp07744d

Abstract:
<p>Norbornadiene monomers and dimers incorporating an electron-donating dithiafulvene unit were prepared and studied for their absorption properties and photoswitching abilities.</p>
