Title: The statistical probability factor in triplet mediated photon upconversion: a case study with perylene

Authors: Lukas Naimovičius, Edvinas Radiunas, Manvydas Dapkevičius, Pankaj Bharmoria, Kasper Moth-Poulsen

Journal/Conference: Journal of Materials Chemistry C

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/d3tc03158f

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/d3tc03158f

Abstract:
<jats:p>Experimentally determined statistical probability factor in TTA-mediated photon upconversion for perylene is ∼18%, predominantly dictated by the energy-gap law. This limits the maximum TTA-UC quantum yield to 9.0 % (out of 50%) for this annihilator.</jats:p>
