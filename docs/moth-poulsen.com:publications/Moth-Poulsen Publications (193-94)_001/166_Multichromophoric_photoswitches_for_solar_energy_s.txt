Title: Multichromophoric photoswitches for solar energy storage: from azobenzene to norbornadiene, and MOST things in between

Authors: Rebecca J. Salthouse, Kasper Moth-Poulsen

Journal/Conference: Journal of Materials Chemistry A

Type: journal-article

DOI: 10.1039/d3ta05972c

URL: https://doi.org/10.1039/d3ta05972c

Abstract:
<jats:p>In the context of energy storage, multichromophoric systems may offer additional functionality over monosubstituted analogues due to their potential to access multiple states as well as having more attractive physical properties.</jats:p>
